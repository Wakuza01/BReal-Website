Shipping Information

We craft each product by order to ensure the highest quality. Here’s everything you need to know about how and when your order will arrive.

Last updated: March 2026

Delivery Options & Rates

We ship nationwide across South Africa via Courier Guy and Pudo, with tracking where available.

🔒

Locker Pickup

R100

Choose a Pudo locker near you to collect your order at your convenience.

🚚

Door Delivery

R150

Delivered straight to your door by Courier Guy.

🆕 Free Shipping on orders over R800

Free shipping applies to both Locker Pickup and Door Delivery for orders totalling R800 or more.

Order Timelines

Because all products are made by order, please allow the following lead times before dispatch:

  • Standard Range Products: 3–7 working days (up to 10 days for larger orders)
  • Custom Individual Orders (1–5 products): 2–3 weeks
  • White Label Orders: 2–4 weeks
  • Private Label / Bulk Orders: 4–8 weeks depending on quantity, formulation, ingredient sourcing, and label design/printing

Bulk & Private Label Shipping

Individual custom orders (1–5 products) follow the standard shipping rates above.

Bulk orders (larger quantities) will have shipping quoted individually based on parcel size and weight. Please contact us for a shipping quote on bulk orders.

Packaging & Care

All orders are carefully packaged to prevent damage during transit. In the rare event of damage, please contact us within 24 hours of receiving your parcel with your order number and a photo of the damaged item. We will arrange a replacement or refund where applicable.

Tracking

Where available, tracking details will be shared via email or WhatsApp once your order has been dispatched.
