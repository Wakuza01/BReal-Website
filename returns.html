Refund & Returns Policy

Your satisfaction matters. Here's how we handle returns and refunds.

Last updated: March 2026

Our Commitment

At B-Real Natural Beauty, we stand behind the quality of our products. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, we are here to help.

1. Returns — Retail Orders

We accept returns within 14 days of delivery, provided:

  • The product is unused and in its original, sealed condition
  • The original packaging is intact
  • You have proof of purchase (order confirmation email)

We cannot accept returns on products that have been opened or used, for hygiene reasons — unless the product is faulty or damaged.

2. Damaged or Faulty Products

If your product arrives damaged, defective or incorrect, please contact us within 48 hours of receiving your order.

To process your claim, please provide:

  • Your order number
  • A clear photo of the damaged/faulty product
  • A brief description of the issue

We will arrange a replacement or full refund at no cost to you, including return shipping where applicable.

3. How to Initiate a Return

To start a return or report a faulty product:

  • Email us at hello@brealbeauty.co.za with your order number and reason for return
  • Or WhatsApp us at +27 000 000 0000

We will respond within 1–2 business days with return instructions.

4. Return Shipping

Unless the return is due to our error (damaged, faulty or incorrect item), the cost of return shipping is the customer's responsibility. We recommend using a tracked courier service, as we cannot be held responsible for items lost in transit.

5. Refunds

Once your return has been received and inspected, we will notify you via email. If approved, your refund will be processed within 5–7 business days using your original payment method.

  • Card payments: Refunded via Yoco to your original card
  • EFT payments: Refunded via EFT to your provided bank account

Please note that your bank or card provider may take additional time to reflect the refund.

6. Exchanges

If you'd like to exchange a product for a different variant (e.g. different size), please contact us. Exchanges are subject to stock availability and may require an additional payment or refund of the price difference.

7. Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned or refunded:

  • Opened or used products (except faulty items)
  • Products not in their original condition or packaging
  • Gift sets that have been partially used
  • Bulk/B2B orders (separate policy applies — contact us)

8. B2B / Wholesale Orders

Returns for white label, private label or bulk wholesale orders are handled on a case-by-case basis. Please contact us directly to discuss any issues with your B2B order.

9. Contact Us

For all returns, refund enquiries or product concerns, please reach out:

  • Email: hello@brealbeauty.co.za
  • WhatsApp: +27 000 000 0000

We aim to resolve all issues promptly and fairly. Your trust means everything to us.
