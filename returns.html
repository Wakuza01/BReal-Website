Refund & Returns Policy

Your satisfaction matters. Here's how we handle returns and refunds.

Last updated: March 2026

Our Commitment

At B-Real, we take pride in the quality of every product. Because all products are made specifically by order, we ask that you read this policy carefully before purchasing.

1. No Returns on Opened or Used Products

Due to the nature of our products, we cannot accept returns on opened or used items for hygiene and safety reasons. Please read each product description and ingredient list carefully, and perform a patch test before full use.

2. Unopened Returns

Unopened items in their original, sealed condition may be returned at the customer's cost. Once we receive the returned product and confirm it is unopened, a refund will be processed minus a 10% handling fee.

To initiate a return, contact us within a reasonable time of receiving your order via email or WhatsApp with your order number.

3. Damaged or Faulty Orders

In the rare event your order arrives damaged or faulty, please contact us within 24 hours of receiving your parcel with:

  • Your order number
  • A photo of the damaged item
  • A brief description of the issue

We will replace the damaged item or issue a full refund if a replacement is not possible.

4. Refunds

Once your return has been received and inspected, we will notify you. If approved, your refund will be processed using your original payment method:

  • Card payments: Refunded via Yoco to your original card
  • EFT payments: Refunded via EFT to your provided bank account

A 10% handling fee applies to all non-damaged returns. Shipping costs are non-refundable.

5. Non-Returnable Items

  • Opened or used products (except faulty/damaged items)
  • Products not in original sealed condition
  • Custom / private label orders

6. B2B / Wholesale Orders

Returns for white label, private label, or bulk wholesale orders are handled on a case-by-case basis. Please contact us directly to discuss any issues with your B2B order.

7. Contact Us

For all returns, refund enquiries or product concerns:

  • Email: info@b-real.co.za
  • WhatsApp: 073 348 9717

We aim to resolve all issues promptly and fairly. Your trust means everything to us.
