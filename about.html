About B-Real

Founded with Purpose, Crafted with Care

Founded in 2019, B-Real began with a passion for natural, effective skincare and has grown into a brand centred on creating refined, results-driven formulations you can trust. Each product is thoughtfully crafted using carefully selected ingredients that are gentle, effective, and designed to support healthy, balanced skin.

Our range offers head-to-toe care, combining simplicity with a touch of everyday luxury. B-Real is not just about products — it’s about creating something meaningful.

We offer white and private labelling services for those looking to build their own brand, along with custom formulations tailored to individual needs. Whether for personal use or business, every formulation is developed with intention, care, and attention to detail.

The Person Behind B-Real

Meet the Founder

Janine

Founder, B-Real Natural Beauty

Hi, I’m Janine — the heart behind B-Real.

My journey into skincare wasn’t just a business decision — it was deeply personal. After owning a hair and beauty salon, my life changed completely when my son was diagnosed with autism. He became my first priority, and in a way, I was forced to follow a dream I had always only imagined — creating skincare, self-care, and beauty solutions that truly help people feel confident and cared for.

Having struggled with my own skin over the years, I understand how much it can affect your confidence. That’s where my passion truly comes from — creating products that are not only effective, but gentle, honest, made with intention, and good for your well-being.

What started as a small, heartfelt journey grew into B-Real — a brand built on care, trust, and a genuine love for formulation. Every product is thoughtfully created, tested, and refined to ensure it meets the highest standard I would expect for myself and my family.

Today, I continue to create not only for those seeking real skincare solutions, but also for those who dream of building something of their own — offering white label, private label, and custom formulations to help bring those visions to life.

“B-Real is more than a brand to me — it’s a reflection of resilience, passion, and purpose.”

What’s Inside

Our Hero Ingredients

We source the most powerful plant ingredients — many indigenous to South Africa — and let them do the talking.

🌵

Aloe Ferox

South Africa’s indigenous aloe — richer in vitamins and minerals than ordinary aloe vera. Deeply hydrating and healing.

🌹

Rose Hip Oil

Packed with vitamin C and essential fatty acids. Brightens skin tone, fades pigmentation and promotes cell renewal.

🥥

Jojoba Oil

Mimics the skin’s natural sebum. Balances oil production, moisturises deeply and is suitable for all skin types.

🌲

Tea Tree Oil

Nature’s most powerful antimicrobial. Targets blemishes, soothes irritation and keeps skin clear without drying.
