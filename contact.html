Frequently Asked Questions

General questions about our products and orders.

Our products are formulated with care using gentle, effective ingredients. However, every skin is unique. Please read the product details and ingredient list carefully before placing your order, and perform a patch test before full use.

All B-Real products are made specifically by order to ensure quality, potency, and care in every formulation.

Standard products: 3–7 working days (up to 10 days for larger orders). Custom individual orders: 2–3 weeks. White label orders: 2–4 weeks. Private label / bulk orders: 4–8 weeks.

We deliver via Pudo (Courier Guy) with two options: Locker Pickup (R100) or Door Delivery (R150). Free shipping applies to orders over R800. View full shipping info →

Due to the nature of our products, we cannot accept returns on opened or used items. Unopened items may be returned at your cost — a 10% handling fee will be deducted from the refund. If your order arrives damaged, please contact us within 24 hours with your order number and we will arrange a replacement or refund. Full returns policy →

While our products are created with gentle, carefully selected ingredients, every skin is unique and we cannot guarantee that a product will suit every individual. Please read the product details and ingredient list carefully before ordering and always perform a patch test. We are passionate about helping you find the right solution — contact us and we will do our best to assist.

At this time, we only ship within South Africa.
