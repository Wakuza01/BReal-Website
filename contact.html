Frequently Asked Questions

How long does shipping take? +

Standard shipping typically takes 3–5 business days within South Africa. Express shipping (1–2 days) is available at checkout.

Do you ship internationally? +

Currently we ship within South Africa. International shipping is coming soon — contact us to be notified when your country is available.

Are your products suitable for sensitive skin? +

Most of our products are formulated for all skin types including sensitive skin. Always check individual product descriptions and patch test before full use.

What payment methods do you accept? +

We accept all major cards, EFT and Instant EFT via our Yoco payment portal. Payment is secure and encrypted.
